Speedcubing

I have been speedcubing for about 3 years now. I first did the Rubik's cube in 9th grade. I taught my friend how to do it, and from then on, we were always racing each other and trying to solve it faster. After freshman year, I decided to learn a faster method to solve the cube. I chose to learn CFOP because it is what most of the fastest cubers use. To do this method, I had to learn 78 algorithms. It took a long time, but I got a lot faster! My current best time is about 14.05 seconds.
